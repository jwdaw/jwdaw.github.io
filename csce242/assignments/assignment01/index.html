How To Get a CompSci Internship

Yay! You're likely a first or second year Computer Science Student and you're looking to get an internship. This page will help you get started on the right path to getting an internship. Dont worry, it's not as hard as you think. Just follow these steps.

Step One: You're going to need to start working on your resume. Make sure to include any relevant experience you have. Step Two: Start applying to internships. You can find internships on websites like Linkedin or Indeed. Step Three: use an incredible service called LeetCode, where you get to solve intersting coding problems. Step Four: is to start preparing for interviews. You Got This!
